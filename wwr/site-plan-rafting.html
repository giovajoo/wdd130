Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide our users a surprising and safe adventurous experience through white water rafting while they are in contact with nature.

Audience

Who are our target customers? Anyone who is in good health from 8 years old and over and wants to try something new and exciting. *Especially to outdoor sports lovers who want to challenge themselves and to be in contact with the mountains and nature. *To parents and their kids, or a bunch of friends who want to have fun in an original and adventurous way.

What will you find when accessing our website? You will find all our services that offer you: Best rivers to practice rafting, high-quality rafts, and river equipment, rafting trips, rafting school, deals and discounts on rafting trips, and more.

How can you access our website? Through your desktop, laptop, or mobile device you can access and find all the information that you need to know about us and the services we offer.
